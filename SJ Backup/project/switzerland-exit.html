Navigate Complex Cross-Border Wealth Management

Leaving Switzerland involves navigating complex cantonal tax systems, timing considerations, and investment transfer regulations. Entering the UK tax system brings its own challenges with foreign income rules, disclosure requirements, and wealth planning opportunities.

At 7-7 Wealth Group, we specialise in Swiss-to-UK wealth transitions, ensuring you maximise your financial position while maintaining full compliance with both jurisdictions. Our cross-border expertise helps you avoid costly mistakes and optimise your wealth transfer strategy.
